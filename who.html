Mayci (also known as mayci_voxel and @MayciTheGal) is an American game developer and music artist. She is active on platforms such as Itch.io and Bluesky.

Biography

Mayci was born on 20 August 2010, and is based in Alabama, USA. She is autistic and openly identifies as a transgender girl. Mayci also identifies as a cat species-wise. She views life with a semi-positive outlook and approaches personal challenges with resilience.

She experienced the loss of her father to cancer on 18 June 2024.

Identity

Mayci uses the pronouns she/they/it/zi. She is polyamorous and pansexual, and she actively advocates for self-expression and authenticity within her communities.

Roadscape Odyssey Series

Roadscape Odyssey is an indie meta-horror point-and-click game series created by Mayci. The games begin as seemingly innocent point-and-click adventures where players select the correct road directions; incorrect choices lead to a game over followed by an unexpected shift into horror themes.

Two games in the series have been released: Roadscape Odyssey and Roadscape Odyssey 2: The Road Extends. The third game, Roadscape Odyssey III: Crossed Paths, is currently in development and is expected to expand on the series’ lore.

Development History

The series originated from a demo made in 2023 submitted for the LuigikidEXEChallenge. After missing the submission deadline, the demo was released for fun on the (now-abandoned) 'Protag Dev Studios' Discord server, but the concept remained dormant until late 2023, when Mayci launched the itch.io page for the first game, and was inspired to make more games.

The finalised first game was released in April 2024. After initial attempts to sell the game commercially were unsuccessful, Mayci decided to make the games free to play going forward.
